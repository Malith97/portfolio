## Field notes
This entry is mostly operational: what the map misses, where terrain changes, and how weather shifted over time.

## Story
I used this route day as a practical journal exercise. Instead of chasing speed, I logged terrain transitions, visibility, and decision points at intersections. These notes make future sessions easier to plan, especially when weather is less predictable.

## What I want to repeat
- Keep the same checkpoint-based note structure.
- Start slightly earlier to reduce low-light sections.
- Revisit two side paths for alternate loops.
