## Entry
Winter running here teaches patience. The goal is not speed every day; it is rhythm across weeks.

## Notes
- Easy pace wins over ego pace.
- Layering and route planning matter as much as training structure.
- A short post-run mobility routine prevents small issues from becoming long breaks.

## Reflection
Consistent running has a direct carryover to engineering work: incremental effort, honest tracking, and respect for recovery.
