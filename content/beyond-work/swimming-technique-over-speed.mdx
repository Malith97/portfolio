## Entry
Swimming gives immediate feedback. If technique slips, effort rises and pace drops.

## Notes
- Breathing rhythm influences relaxation and stroke control.
- Fewer, cleaner strokes beat rushed movement.
- Session logs help spot fatigue patterns early.

## Reflection
Efficiency-first thinking in the pool maps well to platform work: clean design usually outperforms brute force.
