## Entry
Cycling sessions became my best long-form thinking environment. A calm route plus steady effort creates mental space.

## Notes
- Nutrition and hydration timing influence perceived effort more than expected.
- Route choice changes training quality: fewer interruptions, smoother cadence.
- A simple pre-ride checklist reduces avoidable problems.

## Reflection
Long rides reinforce a systems lesson: smooth operations come from preparation, not improvisation.
