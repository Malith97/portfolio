This run started in Oulu during the late winter light, with clear blue sky, packed snow underfoot, and a quiet route toward Värttö. The streets were calm, and the pace stayed comfortable, making it easy to settle into rhythm and keep moving without interruption.

Around the Värttö side, the route opened into wider snowy views with birch trees, frozen water, and long evening shadows across the path. The return to Oulu followed the same steady effort through residential roads, turning the session into a simple out-and-back run focused on consistency, fresh air, and winter atmosphere.
