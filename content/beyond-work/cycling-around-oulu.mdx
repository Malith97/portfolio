## Field notes
I planned this ride as a long aerobic block with space to think. Oulu has routes where traffic fades quickly, and once I entered the forest path section the cadence settled naturally.

## Story
The best part of this route was its rhythm: paved road to open up, gravel to focus, then lakeside roads to hold steady effort. The weather stayed gray but stable, which made pacing easier. I used this session to test fueling timing and found that earlier hydration made the final third much smoother.

## What I want to repeat
- Keep the same route order for consistent pacing.
- Start 30 minutes earlier for softer light on the lakeside section.
- Carry the same minimal kit; it was enough.
