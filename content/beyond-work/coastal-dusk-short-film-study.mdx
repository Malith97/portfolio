## Field notes
I wanted to test a minimal video workflow for short environmental storytelling clips.

## Story
The coast gave a good mix of texture and motion: water, grass, and moving clouds in low light. I kept setup time minimal and reused framing patterns to stay efficient. Limiting the kit helped keep attention on sequence and narrative flow rather than equipment decisions.

## What I want to repeat
- Use the same lens range for continuity.
- Capture more ambient transitions between primary shots.
- Keep shot list short and intentional.
