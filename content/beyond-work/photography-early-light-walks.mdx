## Entry
I prefer early light because scenes are quieter and shadows are easier to read.

## Notes
- Limiting gear helps focus on framing.
- Revisiting the same location reveals better compositions over time.
- Editing is lighter when capture decisions are intentional.

## Reflection
Photography sharpens observation. That same attention helps in incident analysis and operational debugging.
