## Field notes
I wanted to document ordinary streets under winter light without over-stylizing the scenes.

## Story
This walk was less about landmarks and more about subtle frames: footprints in slush, faint reflections, and long quiet shadows. The overcast sky worked in favor of consistency, and a single lens setup kept decisions simple. By staying in a small area, I was able to revisit angles and wait for cleaner compositions.

## What I want to repeat
- Continue using one-lens constraints for this style.
- Revisit this route at blue hour for comparison.
- Keep edits light to preserve the atmosphere.
