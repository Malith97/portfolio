## Field notes
This session was structured around quality repetitions rather than total distance.

## Story
The goal was to keep movements economical from the first interval to the last. I started with drills to settle rhythm, then progressed into threshold sets with strict rest windows. The session confirmed a familiar pattern: when breathing stays calm, the stroke remains efficient and effort stays predictable.

## What I want to repeat
- Maintain the same drill sequence before hard sets.
- Keep rest windows strict; they improve pacing discipline.
- Log split consistency rather than only total distance.
