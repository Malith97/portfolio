## Entry
My best video days happen when the setup is small enough to carry without hesitation.

## Notes
- One camera, one lens strategy keeps momentum high.
- Clean audio matters as much as visuals.
- A short shot list prevents over-recording.

## Reflection
Videography reinforces sequencing: clear beginning, middle, and end. The same structure improves technical write-ups.
