## Field notes
This was a low-intensity session, mostly for consistency and mental reset. The river path was almost empty, which made it easier to keep a steady pace.

## Story
I treat runs like this as system maintenance: no hero pace, no complex targets, just controlled effort and clean form. By the halfway point I felt fresh enough to gradually increase cadence without forcing the pace. The clarity from this run carried directly into a focused planning session later in the day.

## What I want to repeat
- Keep the same easy-first, steady-second structure.
- Use this route before heavy architecture work weeks.
- Keep recovery simple: mobility and hydration only.
