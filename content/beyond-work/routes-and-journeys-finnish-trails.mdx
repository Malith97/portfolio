## Entry
Route journals help me remember what maps do not show: wind exposure, terrain surprises, and timing realities.

## Notes
- Save route variants for weather changes.
- Track start times for best light and lower traffic.
- Keep a lightweight checklist for longer sessions.

## Reflection
Documenting routes feels similar to runbooks: reduce ambiguity before execution and capture what future you will need.
